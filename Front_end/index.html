Welcome to Explore Japan

Welcome to Explore Japan-a travel guide built from experience. Every spot on this site, from local restaurants to unique shops and must-see attractions, comes from my own time exploring Japan. Along the way, I’ve also gathered travel tips, practical advice, and sample itineraries to help you plan with ease. Whether you’re visiting for the first time or returning to see more, my goal is to share the places and ideas that made my journeys memorable, so they can inspire yours too.

Why You Should Travel to Japan

Food worth crossing town for, neighborhoods with history, and a style scene you won’t find elsewhere.

Food & Drink

Counter sushi, back-alley ramen, cozy kissaten.

Culture & Tradition

Temples, tea, and neighborhoods with centuries of history.

Shopping & Style

Niche select shops, vintage finds, thoughtful design.

Best Time to Visit Japan

Every season has a different mood—pick the one that fits your trip.

Spring (Mar–May)

Cherry blossoms, hanami picnics, mild weather.

Autumn (Oct–Nov)

Red-gold leaves across gardens, hills, and shrines.

Winter (Dec–Feb)

Snow festivals, onsen trips, Hokkaidō powder.

Summer (Jun–Aug)

Fireworks, matsuri, island escapes—lively but warm.
